Utopia Plant

If you've ever been victim to a scam, or felt dissapointed with the trendy plant community, you're not alone. Utopia Plant is an online community of real life plant hobbyists who came together to make a safe, reputable collection of plant resources for plant enthusiasts of all skill levels.

This site was created to promote:

  • positivity in your plant hobby
  • provide real tested advice from long-time growers
  • opportunities to buy and swap safely
  • reputable nurseries and small business hobbyists.
