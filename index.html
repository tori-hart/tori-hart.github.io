Universal Design

Early in my studies and career, I began advocating for usability and accessibility. In multiple job roles, I brought up these concerns and educated stakeholders until it became an adopted practice.

At Henry Ford College, my dedication to these standards was a huge asset when Section 508 compliance became a top priority task across the college. From the onset, I was the subject matter expert while they searched for a permanent candidate to fill the Accessiblity Coordinator role. During this time, I drafted policy for cabinet leadership approval, and improved the website accessiblity score to 94%.
