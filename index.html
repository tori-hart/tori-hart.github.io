About

Hi! I'm Tori, and I love to find solutions to usability problems.

I've come to realize that the hardest problems to solve are business process problems, not technical. Those challenges motivate me!
I aim to bring the human experience back into the conversation and create useful, creative, simpler solutions that benefit all users.

Fortunately, I’ve had the opportunity to ”wear many hats“ and work the entire project lifecycle from discovery to rollout and evaluation.

Here are a few examples of the complex projects I've identified and tackled in my current role, Web Content and Design Manager for a community college.

Increased user follow-through

A fresh theme with clear CTAs across our top landing pages lead to increased traffic and submitted applications.

Built content for SEO

Converted jargon-heavy pages at high reading levels into a modular, digestable pages with search keywords as drivers for topics and content to improve SEO scores.

Universal Design

Created accessible templates that worked with our CMS and custom API integrations, drastically improving our ranking with WCAG standards.

CRM data integration

Worked with all departments to organize and architect a custom web checklist for individual students based on information from our CRM, coordinated meetings and training throughout implementation.
