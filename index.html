Offerings (coming soon)

Printable Art Library

Printable Art Library is a collection of low-ink printable art dedicated to people who are in transient spaces, housing insecure or financially unable to keep art where they stay. Its free for anyone to use, and if you are able to contribute art or donate to artist funds, we appreciate you!

Plant Club

Plant Club is a site dedicated to plant lovers who want to shop and swap safely without scams! Create a profile and share your plant joys with other plant lovers on a safe, cute platform that gets better with your help! This platform is free to access, and moderated by real humans. We ask that sellers pay a small fee for their shop pages so that we can continue providing better features and pay our developers for their labor.

Coding for Kids

Coding for Kids is a free online collection of resources that Tori personally curated and taught at a college, and has since grown! Although the depth is for college level, there are many sections geared towards kids of all ages and all skill levels, and a guide to help you start learning the basics of coding, how to build websites from scratch, and information about getting a job in the Information Technology industry.

Definitely more to come

I plan on making a lot of collaborative hobby sites in the same realm of making a safe space for marginalized voices, hobby lovers who aren't in it to make money, and real people just trying to live a good life full of joy, art and love.
