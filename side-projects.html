Human Kind Online L3C is a business formed to make the internet a more kind, curated and helpful space. These free projects are for joy, created with love and a lot of volunteers for everyone to enjoy. They are free and donation based sites to help you enjoy your online time. Please consider supporting them and the hard working developers and moderators by donating to Human Kind Online. All proceeds are distributed directly to costs of maintenance or the people who work to keep these spaces awesome!

Printable Art Library

Printable Art Library is a collection of low-ink printable art dedicated to people who are in transient spaces, housing insecure or financially unable to keep art where they stay. Its free for anyone to use, and if you are able to contribute art or donate to artist funds, we appreciate you!

Utopia Plant

Utopia Plant is a site dedicated to plant hobbyists who want to shop and swap safely without scams! Create a profile and share your talents, tutorials, photos, and shop items with peers on a safe, cute platform that gets better with your help! This platform is free to access, and moderated by real humans.

Coding for Kids

Coding for Kids is a free online collection of resources that Tori personally curated and taught at a college, and has since grown! Although the depth is for college level, there are many sections geared towards kids of all ages and all skill levels, and a guide to help you start learning the basics of coding, how to build websites from scratch, and information about getting a job in the Information Technology industry.
