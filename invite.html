Welcome Friends

I promised I would follow up, and good things take time. Here is a brief update on a wild idea.

Lets save the internet

I quit my job and am dedicating my summer to build this platform and work on misc world changing projects that come to mind. I have a lot of creative energy, wonderful friends, and a talented team that I'm paying for the summer to ramp up. So fuck it, why not?

What I'm offering you:

We are building a network of online platforms filled with real people and content -- together.

  • Wholesome content carefully moderated by people who care sincerely about the topics they moderate
  • Accessible information up to the standards of modern internet and WCAG guidelines for disabilities so everyone can enjoy
  • Opportunities to network with likeminded people and make friends safely online
  • A platform to organize mutual aid, shop and sell, and host fundraisers that doesn't take a cut of your hard work
  • Continuous updates made by real caring people who use the site just like you
  • Transparent operations and code: everything is open source and donations go directly to maintain these spaces. All funds and puchases will be shown & open to community accountability

What I'm asking of you:

  • Dream BIG and imagine a world without marketing, a world without capitalism, a world without trolls, a world where we support each other
  • Give some of your time to the project - as a content creator, moderator, admin -- whatever you have the interest in
  • Talk to me, get to know me one on one. I'm your admin after all and we have to build trust! Take your time of course <3

Are you in?

I'm not asking for a lot, and I promise I will work with your capacity at any level. Everything will be opt-in and you can step back at any time, just 'hand back the keys' so to speak so we can fill your spot with the next person who is interested. You were personally selected by me because I've interacted with you enough to trust you, I see your vision for changing the world and I love it, and I believe in you. I believe we can change the world for better. If we start by changing our own lives and our friends lives we can create something long lasting and truly remarkable. Just stay in touch. Use my secure email to contact me directly (at alwaysuninc@gmail.com or protonmail if you are security minded). Please note most mail services like Gmail etc are not secure and should not be used for sensitive information. I also use Signal and a private discord server (coming soon for outsiders). When being security minded it is important to be aware of the risks and share as little information as possible, especially in written format or any application that records and keeps your content (social media).
